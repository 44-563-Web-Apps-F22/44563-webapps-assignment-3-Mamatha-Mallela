Salarjung Museum

This Museum has collection consists of Miniature paintings, Modern paintings, Bronzes, Textiles, Ivory, Jade, Bidri ware, Arms and Armour, Stone sculptures, Wood

Directions to reach museum

The closest airport to museum is "Rajiv Gandhi International Airport". Directions to reach airport are:

  1. Come out from airport.
  2. Take a left turn until you see a clock tower.
    1. Take right and go straight from clock tower.
    2. Go down the street.
  3. You can see museum after walking 0.2 miles.

Other places near museum are:

  • Charminar
  • Tank bund
  • Golconda Fort
  • Birla Mandir
  • Millenium Grand
