Red Raiders

Mechanicville Jr./Sr. High School is a school located in Halfmoon, New York. Red Raiders is the Mascot of Mechanicville Jr./Sr. High School The school is part of the Mechanicville City School district and serves the district's sixth through 12th grades. In 2011, a merger occurred between the high school and the middle school causing the school to now be known as Mechanicville Jr./Sr. High School.

Fun facts about Red Raiders

  1. In the early 1970's the Southwest Conference created a rule which forbid live mascots traveling to away games unless the host school approved the mascot.
  2. The Masked Rider's horse fell into this rule.
  3. So instead of hoping the horse would be approved each week, Texas Tech created Raider Red as an alternate mascot.
  4. The Matador Band to the Goin' Band from Raiderland because the band was the first collegiate band to travel to an away game.
